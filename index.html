GEKKOUJER
鏡に映した、光の戦士 — "Projected in the mirror, the warriors of light"
CONCEPT概要
Premise

Before they were a Sentai team, the Gekkoujer were a mercenary squad captained by Hayashi Takeomi — six fighters taking contracts where they could find them. Takeomi's crew commissioned a freelance weaponsmith named Yami to build their gear: a set of daggers called the Gekkou Burēdo, each capable of taking a Sentai Core — fragments of power Yami had spent years collecting and reconstructing from the legacies of past Sentai teams.

A frame-up changed everything. Hunted by three separate agencies for crimes they didn't fully commit, Takeomi's six fled Earth and settled in secret on the Moon. There, with nowhere left to run, they were declared something stranger than mercenaries: the Sentai team to end all Sentai teams. Two more would eventually stand beside them — but that's a story that hadn't happened yet.

FORMATION結成までの軌跡chronological
STEP 01 The Squad 傭兵時代

Long before the Moon, there was just the work. Six mercenaries, held together less by paperwork than by Hayashi Takeomi's refusal to let anyone get left behind. Three of them go back even further — Takeomi, Yami, and a trainee named Nanami Takahashi came up together through SPD, before any of them walked away from what they were supposed to become. None of them talk about it much.

STEP 02 The Weaponsmith 武器商人ヤミ

When the crew needed real weapons, Takeomi brought in a name whispered in certain circles: Yami, a freelance smith with an unnerving habit of recovering "lost" Sentai relics and quietly rebuilding them into something new. More than once, when a contract went bad, it was Yami who showed up swinging a blade he had no obligation to swing. Eventually nobody questioned why he was still around. He just was.

STEP 03 The Frame-Up 濡れ衣

Then, in the span of weeks, it all came apart. Nanami stopped answering — then stopped existing anywhere they could find her. Gokaiger's Ranger Keys vanished from a secure vault. An SPD prisoner broke containment under circumstances that pointed conveniently at people the agency already had a file on. A botched museum job left behind one missing object: a ring identical, by every account, to the one carried by AkaRed. Three investigations. One shrinking list of suspects.

STEP 04 Exile 月への逃亡

They ran further than anyone expected — off the list of places Gokaiger, Dekaranger, and Patranger knew to look. The Moon was supposed to be temporary. It became permanent the moment someone decided to make it official: these five fugitives and the smith who'd thrown in with them weren't mercenaries hiding from the law anymore. They were a Sentai team. Nobody asked if they wanted the title.

UNKNOWN FORCES不明の組織
Religious Order 太陽教会 The Sun Church

Every few jobs, the trail leads back to the same place: a cathedral that shouldn't be as easy to find as it is, kept by an order calling itself the Sun Church. Nobody on the squad has worked out what they actually believe in. What's certain is that the Church keeps reappearing — in places, in names, and eventually, in a confrontation nobody saw coming.

Anonymous 依頼主 The Benefactor

The jobs don't stop just because they're a Sentai team now — if anything, they get bigger, handed down by someone who never shows their face and never asks for anything but results. Every contract sends them up against a team or a villain out of Sentai history. And every time they survive one, something changes: a weapon answers differently, a Core wakes up that wasn't active before.

THE REFLECTION WAR鏡の戦争mid-season arc

It takes months before Nanami resurfaces — and when she does, she doesn't come alone. Somehow, impossibly, she's turned four entire Sentai teams against the people she used to fight beside. Whatever case she made to Gokaiger, Dekaranger, Timeranger, and Gozyuger, it's been convincing enough to put all four in the same place at the same time, weapons drawn. The real target isn't even the Gekkoujer. It's Yami — and everything he's spent years collecting.

Somewhere in the middle of it, a mecha the size of a building rises out of nothing — Nanami's, built from something none of them recognize. It nearly ends the fight outright, until something else arrives to stop it: the Kamakiri Mecha, gone since the day it vanished with a stranger in tow, slams back into the battle to hold the line. That's how the squad finally meets Himeno Ran — not through introductions, but through a giant mantis-shaped machine putting itself between her and disaster. While the field burns, Yami finds Nanami in the middle of it, and for the first time since she vanished, they fight each other directly. Neither wins. When the dust settles, nothing is resolved — not the battle, not whatever this is between them, and certainly not what she actually came for.
THE SOLAR RANGERS輝きレンジャーjoin after the Reflection War

Ran doesn't leave after the battle. There's nowhere left for her to go — her world is gone, and the mecha that carried her here vanishes again the moment the fighting stops. Nobody really discusses it; she just doesn't get left behind. Kobo, who's spent years supplying Yami's workshop from the sidelines, steps fully into the field for the first time alongside her. Whatever the war woke up in both of them, it answers to the Sun instead of the Moon. The Gekkoujer come out of the Reflection War with two new names — drawing power not through the Moon's reflection, but from a direct reflection of the Sun's own.

THE CATHEDRAL CONFRONTATION大聖堂の対峙
対峙
Aftermath · Yami vs. Nanami
大聖堂 The Sun Church Cathedral

The Reflection War ends without an ending — no surrender, no resolution, just an unspoken pause. Yami doesn't wait for the next move. He goes looking for Nanami himself, and the trail leads, as it always seems to, back to a Sun Church cathedral.

What happens inside isn't a battle. It's quieter than that — two blades, two people who used to trust each other, and a conversation that turns into a fight only because neither of them knows how else to have it anymore.

Reveal — Nanami A Fallen Gosei Angel

What's underneath the swordsman the squad trained with isn't human at all — something far older, celestial once, fallen long before any of them ever met her.

Reveal — Yami A Gorma Tribe Descendant

In the same breath, Yami learns the blood in his own veins traces back to the Gorma Tribe — one of the oldest enemies any Sentai team has ever faced.

王嵐
Gekkou Seiran Exclusive · Captain's Bond
月光王晴嵐 Gekkou Ōseiran — "Moonlight King, Clear Sky"

Where Shoku's ultimate form is built from collected power, Takeomi's is built from trust. As Captain, she doesn't need all eight Cores in hand — she needs every Ranger standing behind her, and when they do, her spear carries the full weight of the team at once. (The title "Ō" carries no gender here — a convention the team picked up, fittingly, from Ran.) Her suit sharpens into deep blue and silver, calm at the center of a storm only she can see coming.

Ultimate Ability Mirror of the Open Sky (晴天の鏡)

Extends her own Moonlight Reflection across every teammate at once — for as long as the formation holds, the whole team becomes a single mirror, and any attack against one of them is answered by all of them.

Final Attack Ōseiran Kagami-gaeshi (王晴嵐鏡返し)

A single coordinated thrust, every Ranger's strength routed through her spear at once — not the loudest finisher the team has, but the hardest one to survive.

王蝕
Gekkou Shoku Exclusive · Collected Power
月光王蝕 Gekkou Ōshoku — "Moonlight King Eclipse"

Only once all eight Cores exist — Moon and Sun alike — can this form be reached at all. For Yami, it's the moment the outsider who built the team's power becomes its strongest blade. The suit turns brilliant silver with crimson trim, pulsing with moon-like radiance. He wields a massive two-handed sword forged from pure reflected light.

Ultimate Ability Mirror of the Cosmos (宇宙の鏡)

Reflects not just physical attacks but an enemy's own despair and darkness — turning their worst weapon back on them.

Final Attack Ōshoku Kagami-gaeshi (王蝕鏡返し)

An upgraded version of the team finisher — a single wave of purifying energy that clears the battlefield outright.

THE ANTAGONIST敵対者
堕
Former Squadmate · No Transformation
高橋 七海 Nanami Takahashi

She was one of them, once — trained at SPD beside Takeomi and Yami, mercenary beside the rest of the squad through every job that came before the Moon. Something on one mission left her changed. Nobody knows exactly what happened, and she's never said. She just got quieter. Pulled back a little further each time. By the time anyone thought to ask if she was alright, she was already gone.

What She Really Is A Fallen Gosei Angel

She wields no Sentai Core and undergoes no transformation — just a blade and a skill that was already formidable before whatever happened to her. The truth, revealed only at the cathedral, is that her power isn't borrowed or stolen at all. It's what's left of a celestial nature she lost long before the squad ever knew her — dimmed, darkened, but never fully extinguished.

True Goal Yami's Sentai Core Collection

Every team she's turned against the Gekkoujer, every quiet manipulation — all of it in service of one target: taking the full collection of Sentai Cores for herself.
