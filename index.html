CONCEPT概要
Premise

Before they were a Sentai team, the Gekkoujer were a mercenary squad — six fighters taking contracts where they could find them. They commissioned a freelance weaponsmith named Yami to build their gear: a set of daggers called the Gekkou Burēdo, each capable of taking a Sentai Core — fragments of power Yami had spent years collecting and reconstructing from the legacies of past Sentai teams.

A frame-up changed everything. Hunted by three separate agencies for crimes they didn't fully commit, the squad fled Earth and settled in secret on the Moon. There, with nowhere left to run, they were declared something stranger than mercenaries: the Sentai team to end all Sentai teams. Their jobs didn't stop — they just got bigger, stranger, and increasingly directed by forces neither they nor Yami fully understand.

FORMATION結成までの軌跡chronological
STEP 01 The Squad 傭兵時代

Six mercenaries take contracts across the underworld. Among the trainees who once passed through SPD together are Hayashi Takeomi, Yami, and Nanami Takahashi — three names that will matter far more later.

STEP 02 The Weaponsmith 武器商人ヤミ

The squad commissions Yami, an outside contractor, to build their gear. He saves their lives more than once before formally joining their ranks as their swordsman.

STEP 03 The Frame-Up 濡れ衣

Nanami vanishes. Around the same time, Gokaiger's ranger keys go missing, an SPD prisoner escapes, and Patranger ties the squad to a botched museum heist where a ring resembling AkaRed disappeared. Hunted on three fronts, they run.

STEP 04 Exile 月への逃亡

They settle in secret on the Moon. With no way back and no one left to answer to, they're pronounced a Sentai team — not chosen by destiny, but by elimination of every other option.

UNKNOWN FORCES不明の組織
Religious Order 太陽教会 The Sun Church

A mysterious order whose full doctrine and goals remain unclear. They maintain a cathedral that surfaces repeatedly in the Gekkoujer's path — including, eventually, as the site of a confrontation no one saw coming. Whether they have any real connection to the Solar Rangers' power is, as of yet, unconfirmed.

Anonymous 依頼主 The Benefactor

There is no monster-of-the-week here — there's a mysterious third party who keeps handing the squad jobs. Each mission pits the Gekkoujer against a past Sentai team or a past Sentai villain, and it's through these clashes — not divine selection — that each Ranger comes to wield the power of a Sentai Core.

Note: every Sentai Core carries mecha-tier power on its own. This is why a single Ranger — Seiran or Shoku, most notably — can hold a line against an entire rival team without a giant robot involved at all. The mecha below are what happens when that power scales up further.

The six core Rangers pilot the Gekkou Sentai Spaceship for travel and battle. In stable orbit it becomes the Gekkou Fortress, their home base on the Moon. In combat, it becomes the giant robot Kagami-Ō.

THE REFLECTION WAR鏡の戦争mid-season arc

Months into their exile, Nanami resurfaces — not to rejoin them, but to turn four entire Sentai teams against them at once. Her real target was never the Gekkoujer themselves: it's Yami's full Sentai Core collection, and this is how she pries the team apart long enough to take it.

THE ANTAGONIST敵対者
絶
Former Squadmate · No Transformation
高橋 七海 Nanami Takahashi

Once one of them. Trained at SPD alongside Takeomi and Yami, she fought beside the squad through their mercenary days — until an injury on a job left her changed, and she began drifting from the others without ever fully explaining why. No one pushed. By the time anyone realized how far she'd gone, she was already gone for good.

Power Source An Independent Dark Power

She holds no Sentai Core and undergoes no transformation — just an elite swordsman's skill, sharpened by something she found entirely on her own. Background note: she carries Zangyack heritage, though it plays no active role in the plot.

True Goal Yami's Sentai Core Collection

Every team she's turned against the Gekkoujer, every quiet manipulation — all of it in service of one target: taking the full collection of Sentai Cores for herself.
